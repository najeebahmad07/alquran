About Quran

The Holy Quran is the central religious text of Islam, revealed to the Prophet Muhammad over a period of 23 years. It consists of 114 chapters, or surahs, covering various aspects of faith, guidance, morality, and law. The Quran emphasizes monotheism, the importance of prayer, charity, and righteous behavior. It serves as a guide for Muslims, providing spiritual and moral guidance for every aspect of life. The Quran is revered as the literal word of God, preserved in its original Arabic form, and translated into numerous languages, including English, to make its teachings accessible to people worldwide..
